About RushMedz

Making healthcare accessible to everyone, everywhere

Our Mission

Revolutionizing Healthcare Delivery

To revolutionize healthcare delivery by providing fast, reliable, and affordable access to medicines, medical supplies, and healthcare services through innovative technology and a dedicated network of healthcare providers, pharmacies, and delivery partners.

We believe that everyone deserves timely access to quality healthcare, regardless of their location or circumstances. By bridging the gap between patients and healthcare providers, we're making it easier than ever to take care of your health.

Our Vision

The Future of Healthcare

To become the leading healthcare delivery and telemedicine platform in the Philippines and beyond, setting the standard for excellence in medical logistics, patient care, and healthcare innovation.

We envision a future where accessing healthcare is as simple as a tap on your phone, where distance is no barrier to receiving expert medical advice, and where every Filipino has the opportunity to live a healthier life.

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Founded in 2025, RushMedz was born out of a simple observation: getting access to medicines and healthcare services shouldn't be complicated. During the global health crisis, our founder witnessed firsthand how difficult it was for people to get essential medicines and consult with doctors, especially those in remote areas or with mobility challenges.

What started as a concept has grown into a comprehensive healthcare ecosystem, connecting thousands of patients with pharmacies, clinics, and healthcare professionals across the region and the entire country.

Today, RushMedz aims to serve over million users, partners with pharmacies and clinics, and employs a dedicated fleet of delivery partners who ensure that healthcare reaches every doorstep, rain or shine.
